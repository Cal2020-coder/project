What is economics?

Economics is a social science which study the interactions between individual, firms, and other agents. Where these could impact the production, distribution and the comsumption of goods and services in both Mirco-economics level and Macro-economics level.

Mirco-economics -

Studies the behaviour of individuals, households, and firms actions on how they allocate their resources. This is with an assumption where all the agents are rational and want to maximize their utilities.

Macro-economics -

Studies that focus on a larger level such as interest rate, GDP, fiscal and monetary polocies of national or global. In another words, it is the study of the economies as a whole.
